Principles of Digital Forensics and Cyber Law

The following content explores the projects and activities carried out to meet the learning outcomes
This content was part of the 12 week module

Three Major Projects, two Collaborative Discussions and various activities

The three major summerative projects within this module are firstly, the Mid-Module Assignment 1 - Presentation, secondly, the Mid-Module Assignment 2 - Blog Post, The final project is the End of Module Assignment - Expert Report. This section also contains the Collaborative Discussions activities. and provides access to the activities completed during the module (Units 1-12).

Learning Outcomes

This module involved activities, lectures and projects to:

  • Outline the relevant global legal and regulatory environment, including the GDPR and the obligations it imposes.
  • Discuss cyber harms and digital rights.
  • Examine the range of investigations cybersecurity professionals carry out and the challenges of investigation.
  • Explain different types of evidence and evidence collection and the underlying principles of verifiability, continuity and presentation.
  • Examine the challenges and opportunities of regulating and policing cyberspace.
