Research Methods and Professional Practice

The following content explores the projects and activities carried out to meet the learning outcomes
This content was part of the 12 week module

Three Major Projects, two Collaborative Discussions and various activities

The three major summerative projects within this module are firstly, a Literature Review, secondly, Research Proposal Presentation, The final project is an Individual e-Portfolio that provides evidence of work carried out during the 12 units. This section also contains the Collaborative Discussions activities. This page also provides access to the activities completed prior to participation in the seminars (Units 1-12).

Learning Outcomes

This module involved activities, lectures and projects to:

  • Appraise the professional, legal, social, cultural and ethical issues that affect computing professionals.
  • Appraise the principles of academic investigation, applying them to a research topic in the applicable computing field.
  • Evaluate critically existing literature, research design and methodology for the chosen topic, including data analysis processes.
  • Produce and evaluate critically a research proposal for the chosen topic.

Literature Review
Topic: How can technology be used to raise awareness of child sexual abuse material between content platform providers.

Brief Summary: This Literature Review looks at the concept of Perceptual Hashing, how it is used to identify and detect CSAM while also concluding with possible future combinations of new research to enhance identification for forensic analysis.
